Nonso Amadi - Go Outside ft. Mr Eazi: video, lyrics, reactions

The young Nigerian star is back with another banger Nonso Amadi - Go Outside. This beautiful and mellow song is perfect for listening to after a busy day. Press the play button and feel all your troubles fade away.

The singer teamed up with another famous Nigerian musician Mr Eazi. This track is a part of the Nonso Amadi’s latest EP, which is called Free.

Nonso Amadi - Go Outside description

  • Released: August 9, 2019
  • Format: mp3, video
  • Genre: Afro-fusion
  • Length: 3:13 min
  • Label: Nonso Amadi
  • Director: Mollana Burke

You will love the smooth melody of this song. The lilting voices of the singers make this track even more alluring. You do not even notice the moment you start humming along – this record is impossible to resist.
