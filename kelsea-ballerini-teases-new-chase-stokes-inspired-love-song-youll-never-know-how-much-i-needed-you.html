Fans can expect Kelsea Ballerini’s new music to be packed full of love. The country songstress recently gave a glimpse of her new single, seemingly celebrating her newfound romance with Chase Stokes.

Kelsea Ballerini Unreleased Single Seemingly Describes Romance with Chase Stokes

On Thanksgiving, Ballerini posted a series of photos and video on Instagram, recapping some of her best moments recently. The country crooner shared the same alongside a candid caption.

“I’ve got a heart with gratitude that is spilling over this year,” she wrote. “Sending yummy love and light your way. happy thanksgiving friends.”

Upon seeing the post, fans expressed their curiosity over the post’s final slide. The Grammy nominee previewed an emotional new love song which seems to be influenced by her relationship with Stokes. In the clip, Ballerini can be heard singing while back by a guitar.

“To the man that loved this woman after heartbreak, thanks for all you didn’t have to do/When I met you I was jaded, sad and complicated/You’ll never know how much I needed you.”

In the comments, Stokes replied to the singer’s sweet teaser: “Thankful for you.”

Ballerini sparked romance with the Outer Banks actor after her divorce from Morgan Evans in 2022. Fans initially linked them in January when they were spotted together at the College Football Playoff National Championship in Los Angeles. The singer confirmed their relationship a month later, revealing that she slid into Stokes’ Instagram DMs to introduce herself.

As of writing, Ballerini hasn’t revealed any details regarding her new song. She didn’t mention anything about its title and release date. Be that as it may, fans can’t wait to hear the song’s full version.

Her New Beau Can’t Stop Raving About Her

In early November, Stokes opened up about witnessing Ballerini perform in her hometown for her Heartfirst Tour. The actor raved about his girlfriend headlining the show.

“I’m still recouping my hearing from how loud it was … for her to go back to her hometown and to sell out an arena, like that stage — it was the coolest thing. I cried the whole time,” he said.

Stokes went on to share how memorable it was to see Ballerini achieve her dreams while her family was watching.

“Her mom was there and her [mom’s] sister was there, her family was there,” he said. “It’s a magical moment when you see somebody who at 12 years old wanted to have this dream and then to go back to her hometown and do exactly what she set out to do — I mean there’s no way to be more proud.”

Since making their relationship official, the couple has influenced each other when it comes to fashion. Accoridng to him, they’ve been closely tied that they unintentionally match their clothes sometimes.

“It’s been fun, and being able to do events together, get dressed up together for stuff — it’s been a blast,” he said.
