Morgan Wallen (Country Singer-Songwriter) - On This Day

Profession: Country Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Morgan Wallen is an American country music singer and songwriter, best known for having competed on The Voice, first as a part of Usher's team and then Adam Levine's, during the sixth season. He was eliminated in the playoffs but managed to get a record deal, then released his debut EP in 2015.

Wallen subsequently released his debut album in 2018, titled If I Know Me. The album charted at number one on the Billboard Top Country Albums. This was followed by his second album in 2021, titled Dangerous: The Double Album, which broke an all-time record by being the first country album to chart at number one for its first seven weeks on the Billboard 200.

Most recently Wallen has released One Thing at a Time in 2023, which spent 12 weeks concurrently at the top of the Billboard 200. All of its 36 tracks charted on the Billboard Hot 100, a record for the most songs by a single artist on the chart at the same time.

Born: May 13, 1993
Birthplace: Sneedville, Tennessee, USA
