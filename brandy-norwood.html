Brandy Norwood (R&amp;B Singer-Songwriter) - On This Day

Full Name: Brandy Rayana Norwood known as Brandy
Profession: R&B Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Brandy Norwood is an American singer who has sold over 40 million records worldwide and earned numerous accolades, including a Grammy Award and an American Music Award.

Norwood's career began after signing with Atlantic Records in 1993 and releasing her self-titled debut album in 1994, which sold six million copies worldwide. She went on to dabble in acting a while and then continued to release music to no uncertain success.

She was also a judge on the very first season of America's Got Talent, but was then involved in a car accident in 2006. Her brother is Ray J and Snoop Dogg is her cousin.

Born: February 11, 1979

Age: 45 years old

Generation: Generation X
