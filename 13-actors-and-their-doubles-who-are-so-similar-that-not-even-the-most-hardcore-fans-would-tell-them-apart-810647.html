13 Actors and Their Doubles Who Are So Similar That Not Even the Most Hardcore Fans Would Tell Them

We can easily recognize the faces of celebrities because we have seen them in numerous movies. What we don’t know is how their stunt doubles actually look. On the screen, it is difficult to realize who is who, since many times the actor and their double look like an exact copy. That’s why we’d like to present a series of photos of these almost indistinguishable duos.

1. Jason Momoa and Kim Fardy in Aquaman

2. Chris Pratt and Tony McFarr in Guardians of the Galaxy

3. Chris Hemsworth and Bobby Holland Hanton in Thor

4. Cobie Smulders and Megan Le in Shield

5. Jared Padalecki and Mike Carpenter in Supernatural

6. Isla Fisher and her doubles in Wolf Like Me

7. Clark Gregg and Dane Farwell in Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

8. Karen Gillan and Jack Black with their stunt doubles in Jumanji

9. Kit Harington and Dan Euston in Game of Thrones

10. Charlize Theron and Monique Ganderton in Atomic Blonde

11. Mena Massoud and Hasit Savani in Aladdin

12. Zachary Levi and Ryan Handley in Shazam!

13. John Travolta and Nick Loren in Killing Season
