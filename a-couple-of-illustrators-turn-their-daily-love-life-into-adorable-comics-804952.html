A Couple of Illustrators Turn Their Daily Love Life Into Adorable Comics

It is easy to fall in love, but it is far more difficult to keep that love alive. When you live with your lover, you make compromises. However, moving in with your partner might be hilarious as well: you can make dinners, watch movies, and borrow one other’s clothes. Amity comics, a duo of illustrators, knows all about it, so they chose to use the characters, Leo and Lily, to portray moments from their everyday love life.
