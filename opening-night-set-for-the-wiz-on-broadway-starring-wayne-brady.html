Opening Night Set for 'The Wiz' on Broadway Starring Wayne Brady

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Frazer Harrison/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The anticipation is building as Broadway gets ready to welcome back a beloved classic with a fresh twist. The Wiz, the reimagined show based on the story of the Wizard of Oz, is set to return to the stage. This time, the show’s frontman is none other than the multi-talented Wayne Brady. Previews for this exciting revival will take place in March 2024, with the grand opening night scheduled for April 17, 2024.

Wayne Brady Stars Alongside Avery Wilson in The New Broadway Adaptation of The Wiz

Brady, who’s known for his comedic talents, acting skills, and Dancing With The Stars Season 31 stint as a celebrity contestant, is taking on a new challenge as he steps into the world of musical theater. He’ll be playing a significant role in The Wiz.

While Brady’s participation is undoubtedly a highlight of this production, there’s more to the story. Avery Wilson, the talented The Voice season 3 singer, is cast as Scarecrow. Growing up, Wilson’s focus was on dance, emulating the legendary performances of artists like Michael and Janet Jackson. Singing was not initially part of his plan, but his extraordinary vocal talent couldn’t stay hidden for long.

The Wiz is a timeless classic, celebrated for its joyful and authentic portrayal of self-discovery. Songs like “Ease on Down the Road,” “Be a Lion,” and “Home” hold a special place in the hearts of audiences, particularly in the Black community. This revival of the musical offers a fresh take on the story, making it feel particularly relevant for today’s audiences.

While Brady and Wilson’s participation is undoubtedly a highlight of this production, The Wiz is a collective effort involving a talented cast and creative team. The musical’s enduring appeal lies in its ability to capture the imagination of audiences while delivering a meaningful message of self-discovery.

The Wiz Revival Premiered with Stellar Performances, Creative Flair

The world premiere of the Broadway-bound revival tour of The Wiz at the Hippodrome Theatre in Baltimore marked a triumphant return for this iconic musical. The production, which hasn’t seen a major revival in 40 years, delighted audiences with its powerhouse performances and visionary choreography.

Nichelle Lewis, the 24-year-old Virginia native cast as Dorothy, stole the show with her breathtaking vocal talent. Her rendition of “Wonder, Wonder, Why” left the audience in awe, showcasing her remarkable vocal range. Lewis brings vulnerability and strength to her teenage character, making her portrayal truly captivating.

The revival of The Wiz boasts stunning choreography by JaQuel Knight, known for his work with artists like Beyoncé and Shakira. Knight’s self-taught approach to choreography results in routines that feel accessible yet dynamic. With over 20 dance sequences, the choreography certainly plays a pivotal role in driving the show’s action.
