Marco Perego Saldaña’s biography

Marco Perego’s art

Marco specializes in creating sculptures and paintings that reflect an imbued history and today's turbulent times and situations worldwide. In addition, he usually makes his art from repurposed materials.

The artist takes credit for painting the background for Dolce & Gabbana’s Where Fashion Meets Art campaign. He has also displayed his art in various international art galleries, including Zurich's Galerie Gmurzynska, the Museo Tuscolano, Art Basel, and the Royal Arts Academy.
