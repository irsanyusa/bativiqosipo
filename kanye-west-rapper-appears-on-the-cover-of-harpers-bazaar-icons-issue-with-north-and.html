Rapper appears on the cover of Harper's Bazaar 'Icons' issue with North and Saint

Harper's Bazaar has revealed the cover stars for its September “Icons” issue, with Kanye West appearing on one issue. West is set to cover the subscribers issue along with his two eldest children, North and Saint. This is the second time that ‘Ye has covered the annual “Icons” issue following 2016’s appearance with Kim Kardashian West. Kanye’s cover story also features a question about his hopes for his children, to which he replies, “I hope my children never lose their confidence to society.”
