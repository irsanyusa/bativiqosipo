20+ People Who Thought They Were Ugly Ducklings, but Time Proved Them Wrong

Appearance isn’t the most important thing about a person. But many people still judge the book by its cover, and they treat even the slightest deviation from the generally accepted beauty standards with hostility. The characters of our compilation used to be victims of this attitude, but they pulled themselves together and proved everyone wrong, revealing their new looks to the world.

The Bright Side team believes that all people are equally beautiful, and we want to show you these beautiful butterflies that others once considered caterpillars.
