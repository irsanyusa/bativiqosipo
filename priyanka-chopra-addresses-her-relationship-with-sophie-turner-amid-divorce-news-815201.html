Priyanka Chopra Addresses Her Relationship With Sophie Turner Amid Divorce News

Joe Jonas has filed for divorce, claiming his marriage to Sophie Turner is “irretrievably broken.” This raises questions about the impact on Sophie’s relationship with her sister-in-law, Priyanka Chopra.

Sophie had a close bond with Nick’s wife during their respective relationships. Now, fans are wondering if Sophie and Priyanka’s friendship can withstand the challenges of divorce.

Sophie and Priyanka were close.

Jordan Strauss/Invision/East News

Previously, Sophie expressed her deep affection for both Priyanka and her other sister-in-law, Danielle, who is married to Kevin Jonas. The three of them even playfully dubbed themselves ’The J Sisters,’ and Sophie has admired Priyanka since their first meeting. “We can relate on so many different levels. It’s also nice to have built-in girlfriends, who are actually really cool, who I can hang out with, and we can really talk to each other about, like, how crazy the boys’ lives are,” the actress shared.

Priyanka treated Sophie ’like royalty’.

Invision/Invision/East News, ANGELA WEISS/AFP/East News

The actress openly shared that fame didn’t change her sister-in-law, and she remains a down-to-earth and pleasant person to be around. “She’s basically had a 20-year career in Bollywood already. She’s like the biggest thing in India right now. When we went there for her and Nick’s wedding, we were treated like royalty. They worship her over there,” Turner said. “But she’s just the nicest person. We’re all one big family,” she added.

Priyanka felt Sophie was the sister she never had.

Niviere David/ABACAPRESS.COM/Abaca/East News

Priyanka Chopra and Sophie Turner shared a remarkably close bond that extended beyond being sisters-in-law. Their friendship was marked by mutual admiration and affection, which they openly expressed. Nick Jonas and Joe Jonas may have brought them together through marriage, but it was their genuine connection and shared interests that solidified their relationship.

“I’ve never had a sister, so it’s really nice to have Danielle and Sophie, and just inheriting the family by proxy. Sophie is super talented, super funny, and we have the most fun when we go out together. She’s incredible,” Chopra shared.

They couldn’t spend as much time together as they wanted.

The busy and demanding schedules of Priyanka Chopra and Sophie Turner undoubtedly put a strain on their relationship. With both leading careers in the entertainment industry that often required them to be in different parts of the world, finding quality time to bond and nurture their friendship became challenging.

Despite their strong connection, the demands of their professions sometimes made it difficult for them to spend as much time together as they would have liked. “We never get to meet [Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner and family] as often as the world thinks we do,” Chopra said.

Although today she’s an A-list celebrity, a testament to her determination in the face of adversity, early in her career, Priyanka Chopra faced doubts about her appearance. The gifted actress encountered rejection and lost a few movie projects. However, rather than yielding to the pressure, she chose to maintain her confidence and pride.
