Actress shares picture of her cuddled with her husband

Last week, Uche Jombo unveiled her son's face for the first time. The movie star revealed his face on the cover of this month’s edition of Wow Magazine. On the cover, Uche Jombo-Rodriguez wears a purple gown holding her son.

Uche Jombo shared three pictures of her with her baby boy on Instagram on October 7.
