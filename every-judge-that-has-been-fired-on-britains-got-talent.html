Britain’s Got Talent was the very first ‘Got Talent’ show to ever air on our screens. Over the years, the show has seen many judge lineups. Here are ALL judges that have been fired from the show and where they are now. Which judge do you miss the most?

Piers Morgan on ‘Britain’s Got Talent’ (Season 1 – 4)

Piers Morgan was Simon Cowell’s partner in crime on BGT when it first began. Taking to the panel for the first four seasons, Morgan’s brutally intense criticism and lack of patience was to die for. To this day, Piers still boasts about the show’s ratings being the highest in history whilst he was on the show.

The BGT judge quickly made his way to the States to begin another career on America’s Got Talent when it first began in 2006.

Where are they now? — Piers is currently a host on ITV’s Good Morning Britain and a columnist for the Daily Mail.

Michael McIntyre on ‘Britain’s Got Talent’ (Season 5)

Michael McIntyre was by far one of the funniest judges on any ‘Got Talent’ show. Combining his quick wit with the occasional shot at Simon Cowell, it was enough to have viewers at home glued to their seats.

Michael only lasted one season on the show and we still miss him to this day!

Where are they now? — Michael hosts ‘Michael McIntyre’s Big Show’, a Saturday night variety show on BBC One and regular tours as a stand-up comedian.

David Hasselhoff on ‘Britain’s Got Talent’ (Season 5)

David Hasselhoff has fun written all over him, and that defiantly helped when it came to judging on BGT. The actor only joined the panel for one season along with McIntyre but made quite the appearance whilst he was across the pond.

The judge joined America’s Got Talent shortly after along with Piers. They both joined Sharon Osbourne on the show for 4 seasons.

Where are they now? — Hasselhoff recently released a studio album entitled ‘Open Your Eyes’ featuring popular songs such as ‘Sweet Caroline’. The judge also made an appearance on AGT: The Champions last year. (See video below)

Britain’s Got Talent 2020: This year’s judge lineup

Britain’s Got Talent has seen the same lineup for nine years now and Simon Cowell has no intention in changing it. David Walliams, Alesha Dixon, Amanda Holden and Simon Cowell are here to stay! You can catch the four of them later this year for the upcoming season of the show.
